Powers & Responsibilities

Strategic Authority

  • Approve the university's strategic plan and annual budget
  • Appoint and evaluate the University President
  • Oversee major financial decisions and investments
  • Approve new academic programs and major policies
  • Ensure compliance with laws and regulatory requirements

Fiduciary Duties

  • Protect and grow the university's assets and endowment
  • Ensure financial sustainability and sound risk management
  • Act in the best interests of all university stakeholders
  • Review and approve audited financial statements
  • Uphold the university charter and by-laws at all times
