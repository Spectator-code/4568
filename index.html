About the University

Excellence Through Education, Service & Research

Established in 1946, the University of Mindanao (UM) is a private, non-sectarian institution committed to providing quality and relevant education responsive to the needs of Mindanao and the nation.

UM's programs consistently produce board topnotchers, licensure exam passers, and professionals who contribute meaningfully to both local and national development.
